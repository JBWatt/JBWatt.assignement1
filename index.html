Project Idea

“Histrionis” is Latin for “actor”. Histrionis involves a large number of animated scenes eg “A police Interview”, a “wartime foxhole conversation between soldiers” or a “court room cross examination” etc. The game may be played on your own or online with friends, family and even strangers. The game involves a number of templates of conversation. The player is first asked to answer a number of innocuous questions relating to the scene and story. The answers from the player’s responses randomly and automatically generate within the dialogue of the computer animated characters and comedy ensues. A sample scene might be something along the lines of:

Computer: “what was the name of your first pet”

Player: “Bubbles”

Computer: “Name your favourite movie quote”

Player: “Your going to need a bigger boat.”

Computer: “provide a number”

Player: “4333”

Computer: Name a body part.”

Player: “Eyelash”

After these variables and perhaps a dozen more, A random Prison yard scene is generated. Where prisoners are mingling amongst each other.

Prisoner 1: “What’s your name buddy?”

Prisoner 2: “They call me Bubbles and I’m the boss on this block”

Prisoner 1: “Were going to need a bigger boat………What are you in for?”

Prisoner 2: “Bank robbery! I robbed four thousand three-hundred and thirty-three of them. I had my eyelash shot off in a shoot-out with the pigs on the last one, that’s how they nabbed me”

It can be seen here that there are many comic permutations that are available. I envision that a high-quality animated scene would be unnecessary because of comedic conations that go along with having a less stylized product. The dialogue itself would be easily assembled through inspiration from thousands of movies that dealt with common cinematic troupes.
